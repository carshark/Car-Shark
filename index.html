Carshark gives dealerships the opportunity to quickly and easily quote on every sales opportunity that comes up in your principle marketing area.Carshark customers know what they want, they’re pre qualified and they’re ready to purchase; at the right price. But it’s not all about price. Carshark offers customers the choice to purchase a vehicle based on both pricing and availability, meaning that dealers have not only have the opportunity to sell more cars, but also better manage stock.

Customers choose their car, enter their postcode to locate dealers near them and then start the frenzy. Once the customer has submitted their details, the sales opportunity appears in your account for you to quote during the next frenzy. Each frenzy beings at 11am and ends at 1pm each day, levelling the playing field and giving everyone an equal opportunity to submit their best price and availability.
